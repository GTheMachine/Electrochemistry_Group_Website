How to Apply

Undergraduate research positions are available for students interested in electrochemistry and energy technologies. We accept applications throughout the year.

  1. Review our research areas and available projects
  2. Prepare your resume/CV and transcript
  3. Write a brief statement of interest (1 page max)
  4. Submit your application via the online form

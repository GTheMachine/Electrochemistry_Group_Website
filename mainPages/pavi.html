Biography

Dr. Pavi is a renowned expert in electrochemistry with over 15 years of research experience. Their work focuses on developing novel materials and approaches for energy storage and conversion applications.

Dr. Pavi received their Ph.D. in Physical Chemistry from MIT in 2010, followed by postdoctoral research at Stanford University. Prior to establishing the Electrochemistry Research Group, they worked as a research scientist at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory.

Their research has been recognized with numerous awards, including the Presidential Early Career Award for Scientists and Engineers and the Electrochemical Society Young Investigator Award.

Research Interests

  • Next-generation batteries and supercapacitors
  • Electrocatalysis for energy conversion
  • In-situ/operando characterization of electrochemical interfaces
  • Sustainable materials for electrochemical applications
  • Computational modeling of electrochemical processes
